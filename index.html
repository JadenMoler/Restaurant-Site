The Blue Lagoon is a beautiful sea side restaurant which provides the experience of dining at the sea with the absolutely best atmosphere. Listen to the gentle rolling of the waves as you dine on a unique twist on American dining. The Blue Lagoon is an experience that will amaze with every visit.

Our chiefs have elevated American cuisine creating a hybrid of fine dining with foods that are commonly sold all over the United States, styling and using only the finest local ingredients possible. Menu items such as hamburgers are masterfully crafted with the buns being baked daily and the beef mixed with fresh spices or fries cut to order sprikled with our own special blend of seasoning.
